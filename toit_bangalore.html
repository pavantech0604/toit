Kristallweizen

German-Style Kristal Wheat Beer This Kristallweizen is a refined and brilliantly clear expression of the classic German wheat beer style. Showcasing traditional yeast character with enhanced clarity, it offers a clean, elegant, and highly refreshing drinking experience. The beer balances delicate fruit esters and subtle spice with a smooth wheat malt backbone, resulting in a crisp yet flavourful profile that is both approachable and sophisticated.

Aroma

Banana esters with subtle clove spice and light bready malt.

Appearance

 Brilliantly clear golden colour with a dense white foam head.

Flavour

Soft wheat sweetness, mild bitterness, with gentle banana and light spice, finishing crisp.

Mouthfeel

Light to medium body, smooth, with lively carbonation and a refreshing finish.

Alcohol

4.9% ABV
